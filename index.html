Why Problem Indicators Change Everything

Traditional B2B prospecting is a guessing game. You pull a list of businesses and hope some of them need what you're selling. Problem indicator targeting flips this on its head.

Instead of guessing, you identify businesses with visible, quantifiable problems that your product or service directly solves. A restaurant without a website. A law firm with a 2.3 Google rating. A plumber with zero social media presence.

  • 28% of US small businesses still don't have a website
  • Businesses under 3.5 stars lose 22% of potential customers
  • Problem-targeted outreach converts 3-5x better than generic
Browse Leads by Problem Indicator

This is why platforms like RangeLead include problem indicators alongside standard business data. You can filter 30M+ businesses not just by industry and location, but by what they're missing.

Real example: A web designer searched for dentists without websites in Texas. Found 3,400 results. Emailed 200. Closed 7 clients at $2,500 each.

That's the power of knowing who needs help before you reach out. Learn more about building a scoring system with observable signals or explore what competitors' bad reviews reveal about market gaps.
